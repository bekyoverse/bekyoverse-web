Minimum Order — 30 Seconds

All packages have a minimum of 30 seconds. Projects shorter than 30 seconds are charged at the 30-second rate. Duration is measured from first frame to last frame, excluding title cards and credits unless animated.

What Affects the Final Quote

Video Duration

Longer videos require more backgrounds, frames, and animation work. Price scales with duration.

Character Complexity

Number of characters, acting complexity, and action scenes all affect production time.

Background Detail

Simple flat backgrounds vs. detailed illustrated environments are priced differently.

Story Development

Original story from scratch requires more planning vs. working from an existing script.

Music & Sound

Stock music is included in Essential. Licensed music and custom sound design are add-ons.

Deadline

Rush projects with tight deadlines require a surcharge. Always book in advance for firm dates.

Animation Workflow

Brief & Vision

Tell me your idea, purpose, target audience, and any references. The clearer your brief, the better the result.

Quote & Duration

I confirm the package, total duration, and final price. We agree on everything before any payment.

Deposit Payment

50% Deposit

Script Writing

Script is finalized based on your brief — narrative, dialogue, and scene breakdown. You review and approve before moving forward.

Storyboard

Scene-by-scene visual plan shared for approval. Major composition and story changes must be made here.

Character & Background Art

All characters and backgrounds are illustrated and approved before animation begins.

Animation Production

Characters are animated frame by frame. This is the longest stage — no major changes after this point.

Sound & Music

Music, sound effects, and voiceover (if included) are synced and mixed with the animation.

Revision

Final review based on the revision rounds included in your package. Color and sound tweaks only at this stage.

Final Payment & Delivery

Remaining 50% paid, then final video delivered in MP4 (H.264) via Google Drive or WeTransfer.

Revision Policy

Stage What's Allowed
Script Dialogue, tone, and narrative changes. No limit before approval — approve carefully before moving to storyboard.
Storyboard Scene composition, flow, and pacing changes. Minor: up to 5×. Major direction change: up to 2× (beyond that: redraw fee applies).
Character & Background Art Color palette and minor detail adjustments only. No redesigns after art approval.
Animation Production No structural changes. Timing and speed tweaks only.
Sound & Music Volume, music track swap (within budget), voiceover line corrections only.
Final Review Color correction and sound level adjustments only. No scene changes after final approval.
Extra revision rounds are $15 / 60,000 MMK each. Once animation production starts, major changes are not possible — this is why storyboard approval is critical.
