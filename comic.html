Workflow

Story Brief

You bring your story — characters, setting, what happens. It doesn't need to be a polished script. Just the idea and flow.

Quote & Page Count

I confirm the total page count and price based on your story scope. We agree before anything starts.

Deposit Payment

50% deposit before work begins. Work starts after payment is confirmed.

50% Deposit

Script Finalization

I take your story and finalize the script for visual composition — dialogue, panel flow, pacing. You review and approve before drawing.

Panel Layout & Storyboard

Rough panel layout for all pages shared for approval. Major composition changes happen here — not after coloring.

Line Art & Color

Full line art drawn, then flat color and details applied page by page.

Lettering & Dialogue

Speech bubbles, dialogue, and sound effects added. Final review before export.

Final Payment & Delivery

Revision Policy

Stage What's Allowed
Script & Dialogue Wording changes, tone adjustments, dialogue rewrites. No limit before approval.
Panel Layout / Storyboard Panel composition, flow, and arrangement changes. This is the last stage for major changes — approve carefully before moving forward.
Line Art & Color Minor color and detail adjustments only. No panel redraws or composition changes at this stage.
Lettering Stage Dialogue text corrections and typo fixes only.
Final / After Export File format corrections only. No visual changes after final approval.
Each order includes 1 revision round per page. Send all page feedback together in one message. Extra revision rounds are $10 / 40,000 MMK per page.
