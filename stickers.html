Design Workflow

Inquiry & Brief

Tell me how many stickers, what type (static/animated), character source, and platform. Send references if you have them.

Quote & Confirmation

I confirm the total price based on quantity, type, and surcharges. We agree on scope before any payment.

Deposit Payment

50% deposit before work begins. Work starts after payment is confirmed.

50% Deposit

Sketch / Pose Plan

For new characters: concept sketch first. For existing: pose list is shared for your approval before drawing begins.

Drawing & Coloring

Each sticker is drawn, colored, and refined. For animated stickers, frame-by-frame animation is done after line art approval.

Revision Round

You review all stickers together and send feedback in one round. Changes are applied before export.

All stickers exported in platform-specific formats: PNG for static, WebM/TGS/GIF for animated.
