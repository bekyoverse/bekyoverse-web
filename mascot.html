Design Workflow

Inquiry & Brief

You fill in a brief or send references. We confirm scope, timeline, and price together.

Deposit Payment

50% deposit is required before work begins. Work starts after payment is confirmed.

50% Deposit

Concept Sketch

1–2 rough sketches to establish character shape, style, and personality direction.

Feedback & Revision

You review and provide feedback. Major direction changes happen here — not later.

Line Art & Color

Clean line art is drawn, then full color, shading, and details are applied.

Sticker Creation

Sticker poses and expressions are developed based on the approved character.

File Preparation

All files are exported in the correct formats: PNG, JPG, PDF, and source files.

Final Payment & Delivery

Remaining 50% is paid, then all files are delivered via Google Drive or WeTransfer.

50% Final
After delivery, a 7-day support window is included for minor fixes and file format requests.

Revision Policy

Stage What's Allowed
Concept Sketch Minor adjustments up to 5×. Major direction change up to 2× (extra redraws: $30 / 120,000 MMK each).
Line Art Pose, proportion, and minor detail adjustments. No character redesigns at this stage.
Color & Shading Color palette swaps, shading adjustments, minor detail changes.
Final / Delivery Color corrections only. No structural or layout changes after final approval.
Please send all revision notes in one message per round — not scattered across multiple messages. Revisions outside the included rounds are charged as add-ons ($15 / 60,000 MMK per round).
